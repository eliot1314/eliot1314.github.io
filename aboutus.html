Our Mission

At Lexify, our mission is to empower NGOs by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and strategies necessary to navigate the complexities of data governance, privacy, and cybersecurity. We aim to enhance operational efficiency, maintain trust, and secure sensitive information, ensuring NGOs can focus on their missions while meeting the highest ethical and legal standards.

Our Business Model

Lexify operates as a comprehensive resource hub for NGOs, offering tailored solutions to address the challenges of data management, privacy compliance, and cybersecurity. Our services include:

  • Educational Content: Webinars, workshops, and courses designed to build capacity in data governance and security.
  • Consultation Services: Custom strategies for NGOs to strengthen their data protection frameworks.
  • Tools and Templates: Ready-to-use resources such as data protection policies, consent forms, and cybersecurity checklists.
  • Collaborative Partnerships: Building networks with NGOs and industry leaders to foster knowledge-sharing and innovation.

Our scalable offerings ensure accessibility for NGOs with varying budgets and technical capabilities.

Our History

2019

Began as a small initiative to address the growing need for data literacy and cybersecurity in the nonprofit sector. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we gained valuable experience by helping NGOs adapt to the challenges of remote operations and increased digital reliance.

2022

We collaborated with leading firms like PwC and KPMG to support NGOs in assessing and fostering Hong Kong's post-pandemic economic recovery. This partnership allowed us to establish a reputation for delivering impactful, data-driven insights. By 2024, we expanded our services to include online courses, equipping NGOs with the tools to navigate cybersecurity threats and comply with privacy laws globally.

2024

Expanded to offer one-on-one consultations and launch our first online course serie.
