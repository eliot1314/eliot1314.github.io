Course: Data Security and Legal Compliance for NGOs

Our course is designed specifically for NGOs to understand the essentials of data privacy laws and cybersecurity. This course covers:

  • Data Protection Laws – Learn about key regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, and the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance in Hong Kong.
  • Cybersecurity Best Practices – Understand how to protect sensitive data, prevent breaches, and implement basic security measures.
  • Data Governance – Best practices for data management and compliance to ensure the highest level of accountability and trust.
  • Risk Management – Assess potential threats and how to mitigate risks related to data privacy violations and cybersecurity threats.

This self-paced course includes videos, readings, and interactive exercises. It’s perfect for NGO leaders, IT professionals, and legal advisors.
